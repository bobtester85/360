Feedback is the breakfast of champions. High-performance teams and businesses all have the sharp-sword of Feedback very well embedded into their culture. On Wonga 360, you can contribute feedback to your teammates and assist their growth.

How it works

Click on ‘Let’s Get Started’ to identify a colleague to give feedback to, and then rate their work from 1-5, give a compliment or a suggestion for growth, or award a badge. From My Profile, view your own accumulated feedback.

Your data and privacy

Your accumulated rating, feedback, and Kudos will remain private to you and can only be seen by you. By default, feedback is anonymous, unless a user decides to leave their name after their comments.
